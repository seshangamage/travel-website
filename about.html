About Sri Lanka Travel

Your Trusted Partner for Authentic Sri Lankan Experiences

Paradise. Where else do you find perfect beaches, ancient history, vibrant culture, exotic cuisine and exciting wilderness in one tiny island? Come, let's explore Sri Lanka together.

Discover Sri Lanka with Local Experts

With over 4 decades of experience, we know our island home; its every nook and corner. As a leading Destination Management Company in Sri Lanka, our service is legendary and our standards are exceptional.

We are passionate about sharing the authentic beauty and rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka with travelers from around the world. Our team of local experts creates personalized experiences that go beyond typical tourist attractions, connecting you with the real heart and soul of our beautiful island.

From pristine beaches to ancient kingdoms, misty mountains to wildlife sanctuaries, we curate journeys that showcase the incredible diversity of Sri Lanka while supporting local communities and preserving our natural heritage.

What Makes Us Different

Deep Local Knowledge

Our team consists of Sri Lankan travel experts who have grown up on this island. We know the hidden gems, the best local restaurants, and the stories behind every ancient stone. This isn't just our job - this is our home, and we love sharing it with you.

Certified Expert Guides

All our guides are licensed by the Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority and undergo continuous training. They're not just knowledgeable - they're passionate storytellers who bring Sri Lanka's history and culture to life.

Our Own Fleet

We maintain our own fleet of modern, air-conditioned vehicles with experienced drivers. This ensures reliability, safety, and comfort throughout your journey, with no third-party dependencies.

Hotel Partnerships

We have carefully selected accommodation partners across the island, from luxury resorts to charming boutique hotels and authentic eco-lodges. Each property is personally inspected to ensure it meets our quality standards.

24/7 Support

Our support doesn't end when you arrive. We provide round-the-clock assistance throughout your stay, ensuring you always have someone to call if you need help or have questions.

Sustainability Focus

We're committed to sustainable tourism practices. From supporting local communities to protecting wildlife and minimizing environmental impact, we ensure your travels contribute positively to Sri Lanka's future.
