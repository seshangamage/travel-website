Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for the best rates and availability, especially during peak season (December-April).

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options and can help you choose the best coverage for your needs.

What's included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, entrance fees, and some meals. Specific inclusions vary by package.

Can you arrange custom itineraries?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries based on your interests, budget, and travel dates.

What about visa requirements?

Most visitors need an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA). We provide guidance on visa requirements and can assist with the application process.

Is Sri Lanka safe for tourists?

Yes, Sri Lanka is generally very safe for tourists. We provide 24/7 support and local contacts for added peace of mind.
