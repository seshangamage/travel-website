Diverse Destinations

Your 'Must-See' Places

With myriad places to visit, Sri Lanka is a little paradise isle full of surprising variety. You can swap the chaos of a city for a tranquil beach in an hour or less, or switch from tropical heat to cool heights and cloud forests for a few more. There are mountains to climb, waves to surf, whales to watch, history to revisit, jungles to explore, even elephants to meet.

Anuradhapura

The epicentre of Sinhalese civilization dating back to 4th century BC, Anuradhapura is one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, famous for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Lankan civilization.
